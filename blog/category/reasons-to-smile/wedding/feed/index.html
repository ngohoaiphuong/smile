As CEO and founder of You Evolve Hair, Patty Pepper is always changing up her look and is all about living a confident, positive life. 

Patty wore SmileDirectClub aligners before she walked down the aisle during her dream wedding to her husband, D’Juan, last June.  

“I love smiling, so finding a way to keep my smile straight and bright for my wedding day was such a great feeling!” she says. “The best part was it was easy and convenient!”  

Patty shared her pre-wedding routine exclusively with SmileDirectClub. Read on for a glimpse of her big day – and how she got a smile that will shine brightly for years to come. 

Preparing for the wedding

Shortly after getting engaged, Patty began her journey with SmileDirectClub by doing an impression kit. She was able to straighten and whiten her teeth from the comfort of her own home before the big day. She also boosted her beauty routine about 6 months before the wedding with regular massages and facials.  

Patty used at-home mint julep masks and body wraps to help her look and feel her best. By wearing custom wigs from her company, You Evolve Hair, she was able to change her style without damaging her hair. 

“I experimented a lot with different hairstyles and protective styling,” she said. “I love big hair with lots of volume. It’s so much fun to be versatile!” 

Her bridesmaids and mom helped her to capture the final hair and makeup look for her wedding day. While prepping and planning for her wedding, she was able to get the smile of her dreams in a few short months.

The engagement period is also the ideal time to whiten your teeth – put that bright on premium whitening gel and LED accelerator light to work.

Smiling for a lifetime

Our fairytale…6.27.17

The weekend of her wedding, Patty invested in a gel manicure and pedicure. She chose a Cinderella-themed color with silver and glitter – perfect for her Disney wedding. 

On her wedding day, Patty was well equipped with an emergency beauty kit that included mini deodorant to keep any unwanted sweat under control and a small pack of tissues to touch up makeup after any happy tears. Other day-of essentials: breath mints, lip color, blotting papers, and a stain-removing pen. One thing she didn’t have to worry about was her smile.

Celebrity makeup artist Jo Michelle Artistry perfected Patty’s look with MAC SPF Full Coverage Foundation and Prep + Prime Face Protection Lotion. D’Juan chose Chanel No. 5 perfume for Patty to wear – a classic, elegant fragrance to complement her princess dress and tiara.

On June 27, 2017, Patty and D’Juan tied the knot in their very own fairy-tale wedding at Walt Disney World. Their love was on full display in the most magical celebration. Patty’s smile was as radiant as she had always dreamed of, but it was her confidence, happiness, and passion that made that day so very special.  

“Now that my smile plan is complete, I can say my smile is brighter and straighter!” Patty says. “I loved the SmileDirectClub journey and can only speak positives about my experience. I’d recommend everyone try it!”

Congratulations on your engagement! Planning for a wedding can feel overwhelming, but it doesn’t have to be. We’ve broken down a checklist with a timeline to help you stay on track.

(And remember: Your fiancé already loves you the way you are! Show off your smile with confidence on your big day and soak up every moment of your wedding.)

1 year before your wedding day

  • Get the smile of your dreams in time for your wedding day! One year before you say “I do,” visit your local SmileShop for a 3D image or order an impression kit to start your smile journey. Our average customer sees results in just 6 months.

See If You’re a Candidate

9 months to go

  • Submit photos through our dashboard for your virtual check-ins with one of our state-licensed dentists or orthodontists. They’ll make sure your smile is moving on track.
  • Make sure you keep wearing your aligners all day and night (or 10 consecutive hours for Nighttime Aligners), except when eating and drinking.
  • And while you’re at it, now is a great time to visit a dermatologist, get a facial, and amp up your routine for glowing skin.

6 months to go

  • After 6 months, you might complete your smile plan. Maintain that gorgeous grin by ordering your retainers. You’ll want to wear them all day and night for the first 2 weeks, and then every night after that to prevent your new smile from shifting back to where it used to be.
  • Do your first bright on premium whitening treatment. Apply the whitening gel pen to your teeth and then use the LED light to intensify the whitening effect. Results last up to 6 months.
  • Now is a good time to book your wedding week beauty appointments, too – hair, makeup, and any manicures or waxing you plan to get.

get bright on

3 months to go

  • Your bright on premium teeth whitening results last up to 6 months, but with your wedding right around the corner, do a touchup now to maintain those pearly whites.
  • Continue wearing your retainers every night!

1–2 months to go

  • Schedule your hair and makeup trial. Pick a lipstick color that shows off your pearly whites!
  • Get a hair trim and, if necessary, color.

1 week before

  • One week to go! Do your last round of whitening.
  • Take care of any waxing.
  • Treat yourself to another facial. Stay away from any peels and stick to a gentle treatment.

3 days before

  • Invest in a gel manicure and pedicure – it should last for 2 weeks.
  • Exfoliate and moisturize, moisturize, moisturize.
  • Don’t forget to pack your retainers for your honeymoon.

1 day before

  • Wash your hair.
  • Take a relaxing bubble bath.

Your wedding day!

  • Drink lots of water.
  • Eat a good breakfast.
  • Smile! Enjoy this special day and show off your beautiful grin!
