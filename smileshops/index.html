Still have questions?

What should I expect at my appointment?

It’s your first step to getting a smile you’ll love. During your visit, a SmileGuide will take a 3D image of your current smile, which will be sent over to a state-licensed dentist or orthodontist in our network to oversee your new treatment plan. The best part is you only have to go once, and it’s just 30 minutes. Bye, waiting rooms!

 

Is teeth whitening included?

Yes, free premium teeth whitening is included with your treatment at SmileDirectClub should you decide to get started with new clear aligners.

How old do you have to be to start SDC?

Our minimum age requirement is 12 years old, and patients must have all permanent teeth present. For patients under the age of 18, we need a parent/guardian signature on the Medical/Dental History Form provided during the evaluation step.
