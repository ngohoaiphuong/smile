SmileDirectClub Clear Aligners

What are SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Our clear aligner therapy includes several sets of tight-fitting, custom-made, BPA-free clear plastic trays that gradually shift teeth into their desired position.

How do SmileDirectClub clear aligners straighten teeth?

Our clear aligners come as a set of multiple aligners. Each aligner will make slight adjustments to tooth position, a process that is mapped out in advance by a SmileDirectClub affiliated, state licensed dentist or orthodontist who reviews your case. You’ll begin wearing a new set of aligners as prescribed, advancing you to the next stage of your new smile. We make it even easier by shipping your aligners directly to you.

What are SmileDirectClub clear aligners made of?

Our aligners are made of a smooth, comfortable, BPA-free plastic that won't irritate your cheeks and gums like traditional metal braces often do.

Are attachments or buttons required to move teeth with SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Unlike some other brands, our aligners straighten your teeth without the need for attachments or buttons, which are generally considered unsightly or uncomfortable.

How do I know if I’m right for SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Every SmileDirectClub order begins with a free Smile Assessment and a remote impression kit or 3D image to determine if you are a smile candidate. The clear aligner system is designed for minor to moderate teeth correction, including space between teeth, crowding, rotations, and some bite correction. Am I a Candidate?

How long do I need to wait after having my wisdom teeth removed before I can start with SmileDirectClub?

Typically, you will need to wait six weeks after having your third molars (or wisdom teeth) extracted before starting with SmileDirectClub. If your dentist or surgeon feels it’s safe, you may be able to start earlier with a signed release form from his or her office.

What if I still have my wisdom teeth?

If your dentist has recommended removal of wisdom teeth, then please follow that direction. If not, you can purchase our remote impression kit or book a scan and a state-licensed dentist or orthodontist will review your smile for you. And if it is determined that remote aligner treatment isn’t right for you, we will refund the cost of your impression kit.

Am I a candidate for SmileDirectClub if I have an implant?

Our aligners can work for some customers who have an implant. The licensed dentist or orthodontist assigned to you will assess your smile and determine if SmileDirectClub aligners are the best fit for you. If they determine they're not the best fit for your needs, the cost of your impression kit or scan is fully refundable.

Can I use SmileDirectClub clear aligners if I have missing teeth?

Our aligners can work for some customers who have missing teeth. The licensed dentist or orthodontist assigned to you will assess your smile and determine if SmileDirectClub aligners are the best fit for you. If they determine they're not the best fit for your needs, the cost of your impression kit or scan is fully refundable.

Nighttime Clear Aligners®

Is Nighttime Clear Aligner® Therapy Safe?

Nighttime clear aligner® therapy is a safe and effective path to a straighter smile. Like any clear aligner treatment plan, patients should follow their licensed dentist or orthodontist’s prescription of 10 consecutive hours of wear per night. While the average time of treatment may be longer than the average 22-hour a day aligner therapy, this discreet and flexible alternative will safely achieve desired results.

How are Nighttime Clear Aligners® different from SmileDirectClub’s traditional clear aligner treatment plan?

Nighttime Clear Aligners® provide a more discreet, flexible treatment plan for customers with mild to moderate spacing and crowding issues. SmileDirectClub’s custom-made, BPA-free clear aligners will safely straighten your teeth with at least 10 hours of continuous wear each night.

  • Traditional 22-hour a day clear aligner therapy is a more convenient, faster option for customers seeking treatment for a timely event like a graduation or a wedding.

Will my teeth move more slowly because I am only wearing aligners at night?

Each aligner set is custom-made to safely move your teeth within the prescribed time provided by your licensed dentist or orthodontist. Any deviation from the prescribed treatment plan is not advised. 

Is SmileDirectClub the only company to offer Nighttime Clear Aligner® treatment? 

Nighttime Clear Aligners® is the first aligner system on the market that safely allows customers to wear clear aligners for only 10 continuous hours per night. Wearing Nighttime Clear Aligners® while you sleep is more convenient to many people's lifestyle.

Why should I try Nighttime Clear Aligners®?

There are three key reasons to take advantage of SmileDirectClub’s Nighttime Clear Aligners®:

  • Convenience: Customers can get the smile they’ll love while only wearing aligners for 10 hours per night;

  • Affordability: Nighttime Clear Aligners® cost up to 60% less than traditional braces.* You can get a smile you love for a flat fee.

  • Accessibility: Customers using Nighttime Clear Aligners® may have careers or lifestyles that preclude them from wearing aligners during the day. These customers now have the flexibility to only focus on straightening their teeth at night. 

Nighttime Clear Aligner® therapy provides a more discreet, flexible teeth straightening solution for customers who cannot commit to 22 hours of daily wear. 10-hour aligners are safe and very discreet to wear because customers only wear them at night and when they sleep.

Treatment Process

Does a licensed dentist or orthodontist review my case?

Yes. Your new smile will be reviewed, modified (if necessary), and approved by a state licensed dentist or orthodontist. This ensures your new smile is improved and aligns within the "Standards of Care." For more details, read our blog post.

Can I view what my expected results will be after treatment is completed?

Yes, after we receive your impressions or 3D image, we create a custom treatment plan which shows a before and after 3D visualization of how your teeth will move with treatment. If our doctors determine that our clear aligners aren't the right fit for you, we'll be happy to refund your impression kit. You can see examples of how our custom treatment plans work by visiting our Results page.

How long is the typical treatment process?

The licensed dentist or orthodontist assigned to you will determine the best plan for your needs, but we straighten most smiles in an average of 4-6 months.

Can I use SmileDirectClub clear aligners on only my upper or lower teeth?

It’s possible. The state-licensed dentist or orthodontist assigned to your case will decide what the safest and best treatment option is for you.

How do I use my Movemints?

We include Movemints with your aligners to help you get a tighter fit. Simply insert your aligners, put your Movemint between your teeth, and chew as if it were a piece of gum. Use your Movemints for 10–15 minutes at a time, 2–3 times per day.

Why don’t my aligners cover my molars?

Because we custom-make your aligners to fit your mouth, our goal is to make your aligners the appropriate size to realign your smile while being as unobtrusive as possible. Your aligners should cover at least one third of each molar.

How often should I wear my aligners?

Wear your aligners as directed by your prescribed treatment plan. Take them out for eating and drinking.

Will my teeth hurt during treatment?

Much like the days after a good workout at the gym, most people experience some tooth soreness for a few days after wearing new aligners. This is perfectly normal. It is a sign that your aligners are working, gently moving your teeth to their final destination. This soreness should gradually go away after the first few days of wearing new aligners. If the pain doesn't go away, contact our customer care team at 800-848-7566.

Are there any visits to a dental office during treatment?

Most SmileDirectClub customers don't need to make any additional visits to a dental office during their clear aligner treatment, aside from regular dental cleanings.

What happens after my clear aligner treatment is complete?

Protect your investment! We strongly recommend you safeguard your new smile with our custom-fit SmileDirectClub clear retainers. Without retainers, teeth have a tendency to shift back to their original positions. Keep them from moving back with our custom-fit retainers. One set costs $99 and is available to purchase toward the end of your treatment. How do I preserve my new smile after treatment?

As with any orthodontic treatment, you'll need to wear a retainer regularly to maintain your new smile. You'll wear your retainers 24 hours a day (except for eating and drinking) for the first two weeks. After that, you'll want to wear your retainers only while you sleep. We recommend replacing your retainers every six months to keep them fresh.

How do I know which teeth straightening solution is best for my teen?

Your assigned dentist or orthodontist will determine which clear aligner treatment plan is best for your teen. Is it safe for my teen to wear their aligners while they play sports or instruments?

Whether your teen is playing sports or taking the stage, SmileDirectClub clear aligners can be worn with zero interference. They’re made from clear and comfortable BPA-free plastic and are easy to remove. For any sport where a mouthguard is recommended, your teen should remove the aligners and reinsert them after gameplay has ended. Additionally, while clear aligners can be worn during the playing of musical instruments, some teens may find it affects their ability to play, and the aligners should be removed until the activity has concluded.

What foods can my teen eat or not eat?

Because clear aligners are removable, there are no food restrictions. Teens may eat whatever they want, even apples and popcorn. They should always remove their aligners before eating and drinking anything other than cool water. Be sure your teen brushes and flosses after eating before putting their aligners back in.

How does my teen clean and maintain their clear aligners?

1. smile spa™

Our smile spa is the first cleaner to combine ultrasonic vibrations and UV light. It thoroughly cleans all sorts of mouth gadgets – clear aligners, retainers, sport mouthguards, nightguards, bruxism appliances, TMJ appliances, dentures, water flosser tips, and even toothbrush heads. It kills 99% of odor-causing bacteria* with two 5-minute cleaning cycles. The durable, stainless steel reservoir is large enough to clean multiple items at once. Just add water or combine with one of our universal cleaning tablets for a minty-fresh clean.

*5 common odor-causing bacteria: S. aureus, P. aeruginosa, S. mutans, P. loesheii, F. nucleatum.

2. Soap and water

Squeeze a bit of gentle liquid soap into a cup of warm water and mix to form suds. Soak your aligners in the soapy water for 30 minutes, then rinse them under clean water.

3. Vinegar and water

In a cup, mix 1 part white vinegar to 3 parts warm water. Soak your aligners in the liquid for 20 minutes, then rinse in cool water. Caution: Do not use hot water. Hot water can damage the plastic in your aligners and make them unwearable.

4. Baking soda and water

Mix a tablespoon of baking soda with ½ cup water. Soak your aligners for 60 minutes. Rinse thoroughly before placing the aligners in your mouth.

5. Peroxide and water

Soak aligners in a solution of equal parts hydrogen peroxide and warm water for 30 minutes. Rinse thoroughly under clean water.

Are clear aligners painful to wear?

When you try a new sport or exercise move for the first time, muscles that aren’t accustomed to the new movement might feel sore the next day or few days. The same happens with aligners – you’re suddenly asking more of your mouth muscles, gums, and teeth.

 

But just like exercise, there’s a payoff if your teen keeps wearing clear aligners. Taking an over-the-counter pain medicine, like ibuprofen, may help ease this discomfort just as it would ease post-workout muscle pain. Ensure that your teen does not miss more than one day of continuous wear, so their teeth do not begin to shift back.

Why should I choose clear aligners over metal braces for my teen?

One of the benefits of clear aligners is that they can be easily removed, so your teen can practice good oral health habits like flossing and brushing. Metal braces often hinder that process for teens, and they struggle to keep up with proper oral hygiene. Plus, clear aligners cost up to 60% less than traditional braces, and the treatment time is much shorter.

How do I prevent my teen from losing their aligners?

Encourage your teen to keep their aligner case in the same, easily accessible spot and to always bring their case with them when they’re leaving home.
