What are clear aligners? SmileDirectClub doctor-directed clear aligners, sometimes called clear braces, are BPA-free plastic trays that fit snugly over teeth to safely straighten them.

 

SmileDirectClub is the largest provider of doctor-directed, remote clear aligners. Our clear aligners are more affordable, more comfortable, and more convenient than traditional metal braces. Say goodbye to brackets and wires and hello to a smile you’ll love with SmileDirectClub.

 

What are clear aligners?

 

Clear aligners are BPA-free plastic trays that fit snugly over teeth to gently shift them into the desired position. SmileDirectClub clear aligner therapy includes several sets of custom-made aligners, worn for 1 to 2 weeks at a time to gently shift your teeth into desired position. This process takes, on average, 4-6 months. Each aligner will make slight adjustments to tooth position, advancing you to the next stage of your new smile. The process is mapped out in advance, and a SmileDirectClub affiliated, state licensed  dentist or orthodontist oversees case. Every set of clear aligners in your plan is shipped to you all at once. 

 

When are clear aligners better than braces?

 

Traditional metal braces typically cost $5,600 on average. In-office clear aligners average around $6,000, while SmileDirectClub clear aligners are only $1950. We work with licensed  dentists and orthodontists through a process called remote teledentistry. Your doctor prescribes your treatment plan, oversees your case, and checks in with you virtually. By removing unnecessary visits to a doctor’s office, we pass thousands of dollars in savings directly on to you. We also accept  HSA, FSA, and CareCredit. Plus, most insurance companies offer reimbursement.  

 

Braces correct more severe issues, including overbite, underbite, crossbite. Braces can also help treat jaw disorders. Clear aligners work best for those with mild to moderate teeth correction, including gaps and crowding. 

 

Where do I start?

 

If you’re at least 12 years old and have all of your permanent teeth, then you could be a candidate for SmileDirectClub. Getting started is risk-free. Take the free 30-second Smile Assessment to find out if you qualify. Then visit your local SmileShop for a free 3D image of your teeth or purchase an impression kit to begin your smile journey at home.
