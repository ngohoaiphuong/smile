With the Allianz insurance product you pay $89 per month (for 24 months). A reimbursement of $1591 applies for the dental splints. Therefore, a one-time personal contribution of $250 is made in the first month of insurance.

The offer includes further dental services, which can be claimed from the 25th month on. This is, for example, professional dental cleaning.​
