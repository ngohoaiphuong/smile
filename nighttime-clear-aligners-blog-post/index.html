Introducing Nighttime Clear Aligners®, only from Smile Direct Club.

 

Now you can straighten your teeth while you sleep.

 

Book a Scan or Order a Kit

 

What if you could get straighter teeth while you sleep? Nighttime Clear Aligners from SmileDirectClub are here, and many customers will now be able to get a smile they’ll love with only 10 hours of continuous nightly wear. If you’re eligible, you could transform your smile in an average of 10 months by wearing clear aligners while you sleep, and for a cost of up to 60% less than traditional braces.

 

Sound amazing? We agree. We know you have plenty of questions about Nighttime Clear Aligners before you start your smile journey, and we’re here to help.

 

How are Nighttime Clear Aligners® different from SmileDirectClub’s regular aligners?

 

SmileDirectClub’s Nighttime Clear Aligners have an average treatment plan of 10 months and require at least 10 hours of continuous nighttime wear. By contrast, our traditional clear aligners have an average treatment plan of 4-6 months and require customers to follow a prescribed plan of 22 hours of wear per day.

 

Like all SmileDirectClub aligners, Nighttime Clear Aligners are custom-made and BPA-free. Each individual treatment plan is overseen every step of the way by a licensed dentist or orthodontist. Customers will follow a schedule prescribed by the dentist or orthodontist overseeing their treatment.

 

Who’s a candidate for Nighttime Clear Aligners®?

 

Nighttime Clear Aligners are designed for customers with mild to moderate teeth crowding or spacing concerns. Also, the treatment length is shorter, on average, than our traditional clear aligners. Since they’re only worn at night, mostly while you sleep, these 10-hour aligners are discreet and offer lots of flexibility.

 

Which kind of clear aligners are right for you?

 

If your crowding or spacing issues are more than mild, 10-hour Nighttime Clear Aligners may not be for you—SmileDirectClub’s 22-hour-wear aligners may be a better fit. If you’re seeking slightly faster treatment for a scheduled event, such as an upcoming graduation or wedding, our traditional clear aligners may be the better fit, since the average length of treatment for traditional clear aligner therapy is only about 6 months.

 

How much do they cost?

 

Nighttime Clear Aligners are priced the same as our traditional clear aligner therapy: a one-time payment of $1950, or monthly, no-credit-check payments of $89 for 24 months with $250 down. Many dental insurance plans reimburse customers for a portion of the cost of clear aligners. CareCredit is also a payment option for those who qualify. We’re all about giving you options so you can get the smile of your dreams.

Does anyone else offer something like Nighttime Clear Aligners®?

 

No. Nighttime Clear Aligners are only available from SmileDirectClub, and we are the first to offer this exclusive nightly wear technology.

 

How do you get started?

 

Visit a SmileShop near you, or send in your SmileDirectClub impression kit for review. If you’re a candidate, a state licensed dentist or orthodontist from SmileDirectClub’s network will create your custom clear aligner treatment plan. Once you have approved the doctor’s prescribed treatment plan, your Nighttime Clear Aligners will be custom-made just for you and delivered to your door within 3 to 4 weeks.

 

We’ve already helped more than a million people get a smile they love, which is why we’re so excited to offer yet another solution for transforming smiles in Nighttime Clear Aligners.
