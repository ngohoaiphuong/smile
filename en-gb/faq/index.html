SmileDirectClub Clear Aligners

What are SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Our clear aligner therapy includes several sets of tight-fitting, custom-made, BPA-free clear plastic trays that gradually shift teeth into their desired position.

How do SmileDirectClub clear aligners straighten teeth?

Our clear aligners come as a set of multiple aligners. Each aligner will make slight adjustments to tooth position, a process that is mapped out in advance by a SmileDirectClub affiliated, registered dentist or orthodontist who reviews your case. You’ll begin wearing a new set of aligners as prescribed, advancing you to the next stage of your new smile. We make it even easier by shipping your aligners directly to you.

What are SmileDirectClub clear aligners made of?

Our aligners are made of a smooth, comfortable, BPA-free plastic that won't irritate your cheeks and gums like traditional metal braces often do.

Are SmileDirectClub clear aligners trimmed in a scalloped fashion or straight across?

SmileDirectClub clear aligners are trimmed in a straight fashion across the top, allowing us to get optimal turning force to straighten your teeth. This also eliminates the need for buttons or attachments, which are generally considered unsightly or uncomfortable.

How do I know if I’m right for SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Every SmileDirectClub order begins with a free Smile Assessment and a remote impression kit or SmileShop appointment to determine whether you're suitable for the aligners. The clear aligner system is designed for minor to moderate teeth correction, including space between teeth, crowding, rotations, and some bite correction.

How long do I need to wait after having my wisdom teeth removed before I can start with SmileDirectClub?

If you're eligible for treatment, you will typically need to wait six weeks after having your third molars (or wisdom teeth) extracted before starting with SmileDirectClub clear aligners. You may be able to start earlier if your dentist provides a signed letter confirming that it is safe for you to undergo our treatment.

What if I still have my wisdom teeth?

If your dentist has recommended removal of wisdom teeth, then please follow their direction. If not, you can purchase our remote impression kit or book a 3D image appointment and a registered dentist or orthodontist will review your smile for you. If they determine that remote aligner treatment isn’t right for you, we will refund the cost of your impression kit.

Am I a candidate for SmileDirectClub if I have an implant?

The registered dentist or orthodontist assigned to you will review your smile and determine if SmileDirectClub aligners are the best fit for you. If they determine you are not eligible for treatment, the cost of your impression kit will be refunded in full.

Can I use SmileDirectClub clear aligners if I have missing teeth?

The registered dentist or orthodontist assigned to you will review your smile and assess your eligibility for SmileDirectClub aligner treatment If they determine you are not eligible for treatment, the cost of your impression kit or a 3D image appointment is fully refundable.

Nighttime Clear Aligners™

Is Nighttime Clear Aligner™ Therapy Safe?

Nighttime clear aligner™ therapy is a safe and effective path to a straighter smile. Like any clear aligner treatment plan, patients should follow their registered dentist or orthodontist’s prescription of 10 consecutive hours of wear per night. While the average time of treatment may be longer than the average 22-hour a day aligner therapy, this discreet and flexible alternative will safely achieve desired results.

How are Nighttime Clear Aligners™ different from SmileDirectClub’s traditional clear aligner treatment plan?

Nighttime Clear Aligners™ provide a more discreet, flexible treatment plan for customers with mild to moderate spacing and crowding issues. SmileDirectClub’s custom-made, BPA-free clear aligners will safely straighten your teeth with at least 10 hours of continuous wear each night.

  • Traditional 22-hour a day clear aligner therapy is a more convenient, faster option for customers seeking treatment for a timely event like a graduation or a wedding.

Will my teeth move more slowly because I am only wearing aligners at night?

Each aligner set is custom-made to safely move your teeth within the prescribed time provided by your registered dentist or orthodontist. Any deviation from the prescribed treatment plan is not advised. 

Is SmileDirectClub the only company to offer Nighttime Clear Aligner™ treatment? 

Nighttime Clear Aligners is the first aligner system on the market that safely allows customers to wear clear aligners for only 10 continuous hours per night. Wearing Nighttime Clear Aligners while you sleep is more convenient to many people's lifestyle.

Why should I try Nighttime Clear Aligners?

There are three key reasons to take advantage of SmileDirectClub’s Nighttime Clear Aligners™:

  • Convenience: Customers can get the smile they’ll love while only wearing aligners for 10 hours per night;

  • Affordability: Nighttime Clear Aligners™ cost up to 60% less than traditional braces. You can get a smile you love for a flat fee.

  • Accessibility: Customers using Nighttime Clear Aligners™ may have careers or lifestyles that preclude them from wearing aligners during the day. These customers now have the flexibility to only focus on straightening their teeth at night. 

Nighttime Clear Aligner™ therapy provides a more discreet, flexible teeth straightening solution for customers who cannot commit to 22 hours of daily wear. 10-hour aligners are safe and very discreet to wear because customers only wear them at night and when they sleep.

Impression Kit & 3D Image Process

How do I choose between an impression kit or a scan?

A free 3D image appointment can be done at any SmileShop. During the 30-minute appointment, a SmileGuide will create a 3D image of your smile and send it directly to our lab to generate your custom treatment plan. This will then be reviewed and approved by a registered UK dentist or orthodontist. SmileShops are located across the country. Find one near you on our Locations page. If you are not located near a SmileShop, or if you’d rather get started in the comfort of your own home, we'll send a prescribed impression kit to be sent to your door. Simply use the kit to take impressions of your smile, then send the impression kit back to us. Return shipping postage is included. All you have to do is drop your completed kit in the post!

Where can I buy an impression kit or a new smile kit?

You can purchase an impression kit for £39 here.

Is the cost of my impression kit refundable?

Remote clear aligner therapy isn't right for everyone. That's why we'll refund the cost of your impression kit if the dentist or orthodontist determines our clear aligners aren't a fit for you. Simply call our customer care team at 0800 031 8113 after learning about your eligibility, to have your refund processed.

I lost the registration code in my impression kit / new smile kit. What do I do?

We can help! Give us a call at 0800 031 8113 or send us a message. All you'll need is your receipt. Simply provide the date of the purchase and your order number, and we can generate a new code for you.

What if I make a mistake on my impressions?

If our lab receives distorted impressions that prevent them from creating an accurate 3D model for our registered UK dentist or orthodontist to assess your eligibility for treatment, we will contact you to send another impression kit. We'll refund the price of the replacement impression kit at the time of aligner purchase.

Can someone walk me through using the impression kit?

We can help. Your all-in-one aligner box features an instructional booklet, but we also have video support here.

What is a SmileShop?

If you are eligible for treatment, it’s your first step to getting a straighter smile. During your visit, a SmileGuide will take a 3D image of your current smile, which will be used to create a treatment plan that will be reviewed and approved by a registered UK dentist. The best part is you only have to go once, and it’s just 30 minutes. Say goodbye to waiting rooms!

Where are SmileShops located?

Our SmileShops are located across the UK. For a list of shops, visit our Locations page.

How do I schedule a scanning appointment?

Simply book a SmileShop appointment on our Locations page. You’ll be given a few dates and times to choose from and our team will follow-up to confirm your scheduled appointment.

What happens if I am late for my appointment?

Life happens, and sometimes you run late. Please call us at 0800 031 8344 and let us know when we can expect you to arrive at the SmileShop. We'll do everything we can to fit you into the schedule.

What if I need to reschedule my appointment?

Give us a call at 0800 031 8344 at least 24 hours before your appointment time to reschedule your appointment and avoid a cancellation fee.

How long does the scan take?

About 20 to 30 minutes, give or take.

How does the scanning technology work?

Our SmileGuides use a highly accurate 3D optical camera to capture an image of your smile, including your teeth and gum lines. This 3D image helps our affiliated dentists and orthodontists determine if you are eligible for SmileDirectClub treatment and allows our lab to create a personalised treatment plan that is reviewed and approved by one of our registered UK dentists or orthodontists.

What are the SmileGuide's qualifications?

SmileGuides come from a variety of backgrounds and are trained to efficiently and accurately capture a 3D image of your smile. Many of our SmileGuides are registered dental assistants or dental hygienists. We actively monitor and evaluate the quality of their work—including your experience. Our SmileGuides are able to answer any questions you have about our service, our process, our technology, and most questions about straightening your smile using clear aligners.

What if I don't live or work near a SmileShop or can’t or don’t want to go to a SmileShop?

That’s no problem at all. We also offer an easy-to-use, remote impression kit that includes an online photo assessment and dental impressions.

Treatment Process

Does a registered dentist or orthodontist review my case?

Yes. Your new treatment plan will be reviewed approved and prescribed by a registered dentist or orthodontist. This ensures your new treatment plan corresponds with the Standards of Care established by the UK dental professional community. Source.

Can I view what my expected results will be after treatment is completed?

Yes, after we receive your impressions or 3D image, we create a custom treatment plan which shows a before and after 3D visualization of how your teeth will move with treatment. If our doctors determine that our clear aligners aren't the right fit for you, we'll be happy to refund your impression kit. You can see examples of how our custom treatment plans work by visiting our Results page.

How long is the typical treatment process?

The registered UK dentist or orthodontist assigned to review and approve your treatment plan will determine the required treatment length for your needs, but we straighten most smiles in an average of 4-6 months.

Can I use SmileDirectClub clear aligners on only my upper or lower teeth?

It’s possible. The registered dentist or orthodontist assigned to your case will decide what the safest and best treatment option is for you.

How do I use my Chewies?

We include Chewies with your aligners to help you get a tighter fit. Simply insert your aligners, put your Chewie between your teeth, and chew as if it were a piece of gum. Use your Chewies for 10–15 minutes at a time, 2–3 times per day.

Why don’t my aligners cover my molars?

Because we custom-make your aligners to fit your mouth, our goal is to make your aligners the appropriate size to realign your smile while being as unobtrusive as possible. Your aligners should cover at least one third of each molar.

How often should I wear my aligners?

Wear your aligners all the time as directed by your prescribed treatment plan. Take them out for eating and drinking.

Will my teeth hurt during treatment?

Much like the days after a good workout at the gym, most people experience some tooth soreness for a few days after wearing new aligners. This is perfectly normal. It is a sign that your aligners are working by moving your teeth into a straighter position. This soreness should gradually go away after the first few days. If the pain doesn't go away, contact our customer care team at 0800 031 8113.

Are there any visits to a dental office during treatment?

Most SmileDirectClub customers don't need to make any additional visits to a dentist's office during their clear aligner treatment (for reasons related to the treatment) aside from regular dental cleanings.

What happens if I lose an aligner?

We understand that mistakes happen. For starters, give our customer care team a call at 0800 031 8113 so we can understand where you are in your smile journey and our dental team can provide the best recommendation for your care based on your unique situation. Your treating doctor and our dental team may advise you to wear your last set of aligners or to move on to the next set. If we determine you need a replacement aligner, we will send the first set to you for free. Subsequent replacement aligners may be subject to a £70 surcharge depending on the circumstances. Either way – give us a call and we’ll work with you to develop the best path forward for your treatment.

What happens after my clear aligner treatment is complete?

Protect your investment! We strongly recommend you safeguard your new smile with our custom-fit SmileDirectClub clear retainers. Without retainers, teeth have a tendency to shift back to their original positions. One set costs £80 and is available to purchase toward the end of your treatment. Call our customer care team at 0800 031 8113 about 4–5 weeks before you finish your treatment plan to place your order.

How do I preserve my new smile after treatment?

As with any orthodontic treatment, you should wear a retainer regularly to maintain your new smile. You'll wear your retainers 24 hours a day (except for eating and drinking) for the first two weeks. After that, you should wear your retainers only while you sleep. We recommend replacing your retainers every six months to keep them fresh.

How do I change my email or mailing address on file?

Making changes to your account information is easy. Log in to your account on our website or call us at 0800 031 8113 to update your shipping address, email, phone number, or credit card on file. We’re happy to help you keep your contact information current.

Does my purchase include free teeth whitening?

Teeth whitening is only available in our SmileShops, and it is included in the cost of your clear aligner therapy when you complete a 3D image appointment at one of our locations. During your SmileShop appointment, SmileGuides, who are registered dental technicians, will provide instructions for use of the teeth whitening and oversee the first application. All customers who complete this process will receive a continuation of their bright on™ teeth whitening treatment in their aligner kits so you can brighten your smile while you straighten it. Unfortunately, Impression Kit customers cannot receive bright on teeth whitening in their aligner kits.

How can I keep track of when to change my aligners?

You can keep track of your aligners by logging into your account on our website. Click on your dashboard to enter the date on which you start wearing your first set of aligners and let the dashboard do the rest. You can log-in to see when you’re scheduled to change to your next set, which aligner you should be wearing, and how many days and aligners you have left in your treatment plan. If you ever get off course because of a vacation or forget to wear your aligners, you can always reset your dashboard by clicking the "Get Back on Track" button.

bright on™

What is bright on?

bright on is our premium whitening, prescribed by one of our dentists for customers 18 or older. It is a full system of teeth whitening that gets your teeth to their brightest bright.

How do I use bright on?

It is included in the cost of your clear aligner therapy. During the 3D image appointment at the SmileShop, our SmileGuides who are registered dental technicians will provide instructions for use of the teeth whitening and oversee the first application. All customers who complete this process will receive a continuation of their bright on™ teeth whitening treatment in their aligner kits so you can brighten your smile while you straighten it.

How long does bright on whitening last?

A 1-week supply of bright on contains 4 pens (each pen contains 4 applications), and when used twice daily will get your teeth to their brightest bright. The results last for up to 90 days.

How often can I use bright on?

We recommend using a 1-week supply of bright on once every 90 days.

What does the LED accelerator light do?

Enhance the power of your bright on premium teeth whitening with the same type of LED light technology used by dentists. This cool blue LED light activates our Bright Boost™ formula to work even faster.

How do I use the LED accelerator light?

First brush your teeth but do not floss. Then open the bright on premium whitening pen and twist the bottom until the gel is visible on the brush. Brush the gel onto your front top and bottom teeth in a circular motion. Then plug the light into your phone using the appropriate attachment, insert it into your mouth, and wear it for up to 10 minutes. It’s normal for the gel to foam on your teeth, and it’s harmless if you happen to swallow some. Once finished, take out the light and spit out any excess gel.

How do I clean the LED accelerator light?

Remove the detachable Android and iPhone cables from the LED light to avoid damaging them. Using lukewarm water, rinse and wipe off the mouthpiece. Allow the light to dry in a well-ventilated place. Once dry, pack it into its plastic case.

Is bright on vegan?

Yes, bright on is vegan, gluten-free, soy-free, and lactose-free.

Can I use bright on if I have fillings, bonding or veneers?

Using teeth whitening with bonding or fillings can discolor your teeth. bright on does not whiten veneers.

Where can I buy bright on?

bright on is only available to customers who have visited a SmileShop and have had their first teeth whitening application under the direct supervision of one of SmileGuides who are registered dental technicians. Only customers who have purchased clear aligner therapy will receive a continuation of their bright on™ teeth whitening treatment in their aligner kits.

Can I return bright on?

If your bright on is defective, give us a call at 0800 031 8113. We will process your refund and send you a return shipping label so you can send the bright on back to us.

Other frequently asked questions

What is the SMILECHECK™ system?

SMILECHECK is our proprietary technology system that connects you and your prescribing dentist or orthodontist and manages your treatment plan and dental case records. Through a secure provider portal, your doctor can view images and dental impressions, and prescribe your treatment. These tools equip SmileGuides and lab technicians in performing their work and managing your case file. And SMILECHECK provides you, the customer, direct access to upload photos and images, manage your account, and view your treatment plan.

Is there a minimum age required to use SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Our minimum age requirement for clear aligner treatment is 12 years old, and patients must have all permanent teeth present, except for teeth 1, 16, 17 and 32. For patients under the age of 16, we need a parent or guardian's consent on the Medical / Dental History Form provided during the evaluation step to proceed with the SmileDirectClub clear aligner treatment.

Does SmileDirectClub ship internationally?

No. At the moment, SmileDirectClub clear aligners are only available to eligible customers in the U.S., Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Hong Kong, Germany, and the UK.

Do you have before and after photos of patients who have used the SmileDirectClub clear aligners?

Yes. We have several before and after photos and video testimonials on our Results page.

Can I purchase impression kits for friends or family members?

Yes, you may purchase impression kits and aligners for more than one individual. The best way to do so is for each patient to have an account tied to a unique email address, regardless of who made the purchase. While we’re actively working to update our platform to allow a single user account to manage multiple customers, for now you will need to purchase each kit under a different email address to ensure proper prescription and monitoring of treatment. If you only have one email address, there are many free email services available that you can use.

I cancelled my SmileDirectClub account. Can I reopen it?

Yes! If you cancelled your order, you can restart with SmileDirectClub at any time. To get started, submit another Smile Assessment by selecting Am I A Candidate?

I wasn’t eligible for treatment when I purchased my impression kit or SmileShop visit. I’ve since addressed the concerns (cavities, wisdom teeth, etc.). Can I use SmileDirectClub now?

If you’ve addressed the eligibility concerns raised by the registered dentist or orthodontist, you may be able to restart the eligibility assessment process. To get restarted, submit a new Smile Assessment by selecting Am I A Candidate?

What if I think my teeth need more movement at the end of my treatment plan?

We’re here to help you get a straighter, brighter smile. If you have followed the treatment plan as recommended by your registered UK dentist or orthodontist and at the end of treatment, you don’t achieve a straighter smile or the results you had hoped for, please contact us so your registered UK dentist can re-evaluate your results. If your re-evaluation is approved, you may be eligible for additional aligners to continue your journey. Learn more about our Club Warranty on the Returns page.
