Your safety is our first priority.

We're taking extra precautions to ensure your health and safety:

 

  • We take our own temperature every day and will check yours upon arrival. 

 

  • We sanitize our hands and wear masks, face shields, and gloves at all times

 

  • We will have additional masks available for you to wear when required by local ordinances.

 

  • In the scan suite, we'll sanitize again - from fingertips to elbows.

 

  • Between appointments we sanitize scanners, iPads, chairs, door handles - anything you might touch.

 

Don’t want to wait? Get started today with an at-home impression kit, free with reward. Shipping is free as well.

 

Thanks for your understanding. Stay safe & healthy.
